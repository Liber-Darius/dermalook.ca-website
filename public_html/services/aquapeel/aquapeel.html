Overview

The Aquapeel treatment combines mechanical and chemical peeling to gently cleanse, hydrate, extract, and exfoliate skin.
Here you get a painless extraction and exfoliation of whiteheads, blackheads and clogged pores with the non-laser skin resurfacing treatment. The process also provides your skin with anti-oxidant protection. The result is cleaner, fresher looking skin.

Is this for me?

Suitable candidates for Aquapeel include those with acne-prone skin whether that is dry, oily or normal skin.

How long does it take and how often can I have it done?

The procedure takes from 40-50 minutes. It is recommended to have at least 2 weeks interval in between procedures.

How long is the downtime?

No downtime.
