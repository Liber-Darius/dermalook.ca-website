About us

Welcome to DermalookSkin care & Laser clinic-a professional skin care clinic operating in Toronto. Our Clinic provides clients in Toronto with the highest level of patient skin care and the most advanced skin care treatments.
We offer state-of-the-art skin care treatments to resolve any skin problem you may have, whether you are looking for acne treatment, anti-aging, pigmentation, hair removal or any other skin problem solution, our clinic can help you get smooth, beautiful and healthy skin.
We provide professional skin care to enhance your skin and keep it healthy and beautiful: skin tightening, Oxygeneo Treatments, hair removal, natural herbal peels, microdermabrasion, dermaplaning, acne scar treatments, microneedling, antiaging and many more.
We are proud to offer the most effective skin care treatments available in Toronto
